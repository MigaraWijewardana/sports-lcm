WHAT IS
SPORTS LCM?

This is your chance to awaken your competitiveness.

AIESEC focuses on holistic development by fostering teamwork, healthy competition, and well-being among its members. We have decided to organize a Sports Local Committee Meeting with the purpose of bringing together AIESECers from all backgrounds to engage in physical activities, strengthen team dynamics, build long-lasting camaraderie, and celebrate the spirit of sportsmanship within our network.

VISION

To create an energetic and inclusive environment where every member feels empowered to participate, connect, and collaborate through the power of sports. We aim to break down barriers, boost committee morale, and ignite a lasting sense of passion and unity within our Local Committee.

MISSION

To organize a memorable, high-energy, and inclusive sports day that encourages active participation from all members, promotes physical well-being and strategic thinking and provides a dynamic platform for leadership development and relationship building.
